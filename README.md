GlowGuide: Smart Beauty Calendar & Care Assistant
GlowGuide is a mobile assistant designed to digitize cosmetic product tracking for modern consumers while leveraging artificial intelligence to analyze product ingredients. More than just a calendar, it also serves as a social platform where users can build communities based on their skin types.

✨ The Inspiration Behind the Project

Many users struggle to track the expiration dates (PAO – Period After Opening) of the cosmetic products they purchase and often find it difficult to understand complex chemical ingredient names. GlowGuide aims to simplify this complexity by utilizing OCR (Optical Character Recognition) and Artificial Intelligence.

🚀 Core Planned Features
-AI Label Analysis:Instant detection of potentially harmful ingredients by simply taking a photo of the product’s ingredient list.
-Dynamic Calendar:Automatic calculation and reminders for product expiration dates after opening.
-Live Support Rooms: Real-time chat spaces for users with similar skin types to connect and share experiences.
-Smart Assistant:Personalized daily skincare routine recommendations based on weather conditions (humidity/temperature).
